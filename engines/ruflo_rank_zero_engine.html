The hierarchy of dominance (2026)
Layer 1: Utility — The Answer Engine
Does the user get their answer in < 3 seconds? First paragraph must directly answer the query. 40–60 word direct answer block.
Layer 2: Authority — The E-E-A-T Engine
Why should Google trust you over Wikipedia? Lived experience, verifiable credentials, real citations, honest limitations.
Layer 3: Comprehensiveness — The Semantic Engine
Have you covered the "unknown unknowns"? Entity density, topical authority, LSI keywords, question clustering.
Layer 4: Extractability — The AI/LLM Engine (GEO)
Is your data formatted so Perplexity/ChatGPT/Gemini can scrape and cite it? Tables, structured lists, definition paragraphs, bold takeaways.
